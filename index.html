About us

The Google Developer Group Dhaka is a local groups of developers who are specifically interested in Google products and APIs. We get togather to watch our latest videos, Services, hearing to large gatherings with demos and tech talks, to hackathons, we talk about the grouding up global opportunities in technology industry.

GDG Dhaka is run by passionate Managers, Organizers and Ambassadors in Bangladesh. GDG Dhaka is a group to share ideas and network with fellow developers, a place to learn about Google technologies and development tools, as well as what local companies and developers are doing with these technologies, focused on developers and educational technical content, open to and free for all interested
